About me

Web Developer

As a MEAN stack developer with over two years of experience at Strands Energy Pvt Ltd, I have worked on a wide range of projects that showcase my expertise in both backend and frontend development. I have been part of a team that designed and implemented a cloud-based web application for our product, where I played a key role in building the backend using Node.js, Express.js, and PostgreSQL. I have also developed external APIs, allowing integration of our product with third-party tools through a token-based authentication mechanism, which expanded our customer base. In addition, I worked on scaling our database with PostgreSQL, using techniques such as table partitioning and Citus to distribute tables across machines. I contributed to the development of a 'Schedule & Dispatch' application for managing schedules and dispatching commands to electrical devices. My work also includes building an internal API gateway to communicate with various electrical grids, along with developing multiple UI components using Angular for our web application.

Self Made Project
